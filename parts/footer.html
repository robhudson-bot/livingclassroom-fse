The Living Classroom programme is delivered across the traditional, ancestral, and treaty lands of Indigenous peoples throughout Ontario. We acknowledge the Anishinaabe, Haudenosaunee, and Attawandaron (Neutral) peoples on whose territory the RIA is located, and we commit to learning from Indigenous ways of knowing in long-term care.
